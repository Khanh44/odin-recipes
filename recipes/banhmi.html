Description

Banh Mi a Vietnamese Submarine is filled with many custimizable ingredients.

The key to a good banh mi is fresh ingredients and crispy yet soft bread.

Ingredients

  • pate
  • mayo
  • cucumber
  • crusty bread
  • green onion
  • cilantro
  • chilli
  • carrot
  • Cold cut meat

Steps

  1. Open crusty bread slightly
  2. Spread mayo inside bread
  3. Spread pate
  4. Add cold cut meat
  5. Add green onion, carrot and cucumber
  6. Add cilantro and chilli as desired
