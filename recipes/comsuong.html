Description

Com Suong is broken rice served with grilled pork chops

Proteins that can be added are grilled chicken, fried eggs, and pig skin.

Ingredients

  • pork chop
  • broken rice
  • soy sauce
  • brown sugar
  • lemongrass
  • cucumber
  • tomatoes
  • fried egg
  • pickled carrots

Steps

  1. Marinate pork chop with soy sauce and brown sugar.
  2. Leave in fridge to marinate for at least 2 hours.
  3. Grease the grill pan with oil and heat it up.
  4. Transfer the pork chops onto the pan and grill till the meat caramelises and turns slightly charred on both sides.
  5. Cook the broken rice.
  6. Transfer the broken rice and pork chops onto a dish.
  7. Fry egg and add it to the dish.
  8. Add pickled carrots, tomatoes, and cucumber to the dish.
  9. Mix fish sauce with some chilli in a small bowl.
