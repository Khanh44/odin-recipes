Description

Pho is a soup broth filled with assortment of meats, fresh herbs and is filling.

The ingredients to making pho can be customized to our personal taste.

Ingredients

  • beef
  • fresh herbs
  • pho noodles
  • lime
  • celantro
  • beef tripe
  • pho broth

Steps

  1. make pho broth
  2. cook pho noodles seperately
  3. mix in bones and meat to pho broth
  4. add fresh herbs to pho broth
  5. add celantro
  6. mix in pho noodles with beef broth
  7. squeeze lime
